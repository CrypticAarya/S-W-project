1. Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 red onion (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon juice (for dressing)
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Spread the bell pepper, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and onion on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  3. Meanwhile, cook quinoa. Combine the quinoa and water in a medium pot, bring to a boil, then lower the heat and simmer for about 15 minutes until the water is absorbed and the quinoa is cooked.
  4. Once everything is ready, toss the quinoa with the roasted vegetables, add fresh lemon juice, and garnish with chopped parsley. Serve warm or chilled.

2. Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread (toasted)
  • 1 ripe avocado (smashed)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar (for poaching eggs)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a simmer and add apple cider vinegar. Crack eggs into individual bowls.
  2. Create a whirlpool in the simmering water and gently slide in the eggs. Poach for 3-4 minutes until whites are set but yolks are runny.
  3. While the eggs poach, spread the smashed avocado on the toasted bread and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Top each slice with a poached egg and garnish with fresh herbs.

3. Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch piece ginger (grated)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, and ginger, and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
  2. Add curry powder and cook for another minute, then add chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and coconut milk. Stir well and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with brown rice or whole-grain naan.

4. Greek Yogurt Parfait with Berries and Nuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain, unsweetened)
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons granola
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pistachios)

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, berries, and granola.
  2. Drizzle with honey if you want added sweetness.
  3. Top with chopped nuts and enjoy as a snack or breakfast.

5. Sweet Potato and Black Bean Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (peeled and diced)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans (rinsed and drained)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • Fresh lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the diced sweet potatoes with olive oil, cumin, and chili powder. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  3. Assemble tacos by layering roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, shredded lettuce, and cilantro.
  4. Serve with lime wedges and enjoy.
